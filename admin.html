How This Dashboard Works
Driver App
Drivers use a mobile app (/driver) that shows their route and stops. When a driver has the app open, their shuttle shows as "Online" with their current stop, lap count, and route type. The app reports every 30 seconds.
Automated Event Logging
The main tracker (/tracker) automatically detects when a shuttle arrives at, departs from, or skips a stop using GPS proximity. These events feed into the stats, performance tables, and lap tracking below.
Day vs Night Route
At 7 PM CT the route switches from Day (11 stops) to Night (13 stops), adding Hotel Vegas and Shangri-La. The route chip in the top bar shows which mode is active.
Fleet Status
Each shuttle card shows its status: Online (driver app active in last 5 min), Away (5-60 min since last check-in), or Offline. Data comes from the driver app and logged events in the database.
